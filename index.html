Flashcard Fun

A fun game that helps build multiplication logic.

I made this fun app to teach my small children how to multiply. Instead of having index cards all over the floor, this both taught them how to mulitply and keep our livingroom floor clean. The challenge I game myself was to make it as lightweight as possible, with no frameworks. I made custom sounds, experimented with Adobe Illustrator, and researched the benefits of studying math logic with classical music to engage both the left and right brain. Best of all, the kids love it.
